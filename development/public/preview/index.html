We're back

Join us from 17th-18th November 2018 at the Calman Learning Centre, Durham University for the third edition of DurHack. This year, hackers from Durham and around the world will be pushing the boundaries of technology to new limits.

Last year, we brought students together from numerous universities, to create everything from a waiter Alexa skill for ordering food at a restaurant, a traffic simulation using Java and node.js, and a Google Maps heatmap showing the safest routes home after a night out.

Make sure to check out DurHack 2017’s photos and submissions!

For general enquiries, email hello@durhack.com.
